# Product Requirements Document

## Executive Summary

CloudFolio is a professional engineering platform for Narendra Pratap Singh, a cloud and DevOps engineer with 14+ years of experience. The product will help communicate senior-level capability for international roles in cloud infrastructure, SRE, platform engineering, DevOps, and AWS engineering.

CloudFolio will start as a content-rich portfolio and expand into an interactive platform with recruiter workflows, architecture visualizations, project evidence, and future AI-assisted navigation.

## Problem Statement

Senior infrastructure candidates are often evaluated through fragmented signals: resumes, LinkedIn profiles, GitHub repositories, certifications, and interview conversations. These channels rarely show the full engineering story.

CloudFolio will consolidate those signals into one platform that presents technical depth, operational maturity, communication ability, and career readiness.

## Goals

- Communicate Narendra's senior engineering profile clearly and quickly.
- Present projects, case studies, architecture, and operational practices with measurable impact.
- Support recruiter and hiring manager workflows.
- Build trust through transparent documentation and clean engineering standards.
- Keep the platform extensible for future content, integrations, and AI features.

## Non-goals

- Do not replace the formal resume or LinkedIn profile.
- Do not expose confidential employer, client, or infrastructure information.
- Do not create a generic template portfolio.
- Do not optimize for visual novelty at the expense of credibility.
- Do not introduce implementation dependencies before the project bootstrap milestone.

## User Personas

### Recruiter

Needs to understand role fit, location readiness, availability, seniority, contact options, and resume details quickly.

### Hiring Manager

Needs evidence of practical experience, project ownership, architectural thinking, reliability mindset, and communication quality.

### Senior Engineer or Interviewer

Needs to assess technical depth, tradeoffs, infrastructure design, incident thinking, automation practices, and code quality.

### General Visitor

Needs a clear understanding of who Narendra is, what he builds, and how to navigate the platform.

## Functional Requirements

- Home page with positioning, role focus, and primary calls to action.
- About page with professional background, strengths, and career narrative.
- Interactive resume with experience, skills, achievements, and downloadable resume.
- Projects section with technical details, links, outcomes, and stack information.
- Case studies with problem, constraints, decisions, implementation, results, and lessons.
- Technical blog for cloud, DevOps, SRE, platform engineering, Linux, AWS, Kubernetes, Terraform, and automation topics.
- Architecture gallery for diagrams and system explanations.
- Certifications section with credentials, verification links, and learning context.
- Career timeline with experience, milestones, and growth.
- Recruiter hub with role targeting, location preferences, resume, contact, and hiring signals.
- Contact platform with clear channels and future form support.
- Future support for GitHub, analytics, content search, and AI assistant integrations.

## Non-functional Requirements

- Fast page loads and strong Core Web Vitals.
- Accessible navigation, contrast, headings, keyboard behavior, and semantic structure.
- Responsive layouts across mobile, tablet, desktop, and wide screens.
- Secure handling of contact forms, environment variables, and integrations.
- SEO-friendly metadata, structured data, and indexable content.
- Maintainable content structure and version-controlled documentation.
- International-friendly language and presentation.
- Privacy-conscious analytics.

## Risks

- Scope expansion could delay the first usable release.
- Overly complex visuals could reduce clarity for recruiters.
- Public case studies must avoid confidential information.
- Content volume may become difficult to maintain without clear structure.
- Future integrations may introduce privacy, security, or reliability concerns.

## Future Enhancements

- AI assistant for recruiters and visitors.
- Personalized recruiter landing pages.
- Search across projects, posts, architecture, and resume content.
- GitHub contribution widgets.
- Certification verification integrations.
- Downloadable role-specific resume variants.
- Multilingual pages for target markets.
- Analytics dashboard for career marketing performance.
