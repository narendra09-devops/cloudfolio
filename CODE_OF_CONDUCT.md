# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

CloudFolio is intended to be a respectful, professional, and inclusive open-source project.

Project maintainers and contributors are expected to create a constructive environment for people of all backgrounds, experience levels, and perspectives.

## Expected Behavior

- Communicate respectfully and professionally.
- Give and receive feedback with clarity and good intent.
- Focus criticism on ideas, implementation, and outcomes.
- Respect different experience levels and learning paths.
- Help keep discussions productive and relevant.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory language
- Personal attacks or hostile comments
- Dismissive or abusive behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any conduct that would reasonably make participation unsafe or unwelcome

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or pull requests, restrict participation, or take other appropriate action when behavior violates this code of conduct.

Concerns can be reported through the contact process described in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) or through project maintainer channels.
